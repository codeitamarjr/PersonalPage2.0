    About me

    I am Itamar Junior, a proud graduate with a BSc in Computing (with Honours of Distinction) from Dorset College in Dublin.

    I am 34 years old, originally from Brazil. I relocated to Ireland in May of 2018 in search of new adventures, a deeper understanding of the English language, and opportunities to further hone my skills.

    My passion for technology is evident not just in my academic achievements but also in real-world applications. A project I worked on during my studies has been adopted and is now widely used by a prominent company.

    Outside of my professional endeavors, I am an avid gamer and enjoy keeping fit at the gym. Social connections matter to me, so I often spend time with friends and indulge in photography. To see some of my shots, do check out my Instagram link in the Contact section.

    To give back to the community and share my experiences, I've started a YouTube channel and a blog. Through these platforms, I provide insights about life in Ireland, especially catering to Brazilians interested in relocating here.

    All links to my projects and endeavors can be found below on this page.
